Word Potato!





How to Play

  1. Determine how many players are on each team.
  2. Designate a clue giver for each each team to handle the device
  3. Each round is two minutes in length, with the number of rounds determined by the number of players.
  4. Each clue master will be given a word to describe to their team, but can not say the word itself.
  5. Once the word has been guessed correctly, quickly pass the device to the other clue master.
  6. The last person to have the device when the timer goes off, loses the round and the other team gets a point.
  7. Team with the most points after all rounds completed wins.
